About Us

TRUST

built on facts

At Automotores Martino we have spent over 15 years connecting people with the car they need. We were born in Córdoba with one conviction: buying a car should be a transparent, honest, and surprise-free experience.

Every vehicle in our stock goes through a rigorous technical inspection. All paperwork is in order. We don't just sell you a car — we give you the peace of mind of knowing exactly what you're buying.

Whether you're a first-time driver or looking to upgrade your fleet, at Automotores Martino you'll find personalized service, fair prices, and the backing of a company that values its reputation above all else.
